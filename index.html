    Host‑Your‑Own Tutoring Hour (Toolkit)

    1. Pick a weekly slot (e.g., Tue 6–7p) and location (library study room or Zoom).
    2. Focus: homework help + fundamentals (fractions/decimals, algebra basics, reading fluency).
    3. Bring: whiteboard, simple manipulatives, scratch paper; keep it upbeat and encouraging.
    4. Promote: share the Events link below; invite parents via church/community groups.
    5. Safety: never 1:1 in closed spaces; keep sessions visible and adhere to site rules.

    Faith‑Friendly Service Ideas

    • • Sponsor a weekly homework & pizza night at church for teens.
    • • Start a coding club (micro‑projects in Python/robotics) for grades 6–10.
    • • Pair mentors with Boys & Girls Club teens on resume & interview basics.
    • • Organize Saturday nature cleanups at the Riparian Preserve with families.

    About this project

    This hub is a simple, volunteer‑run directory to help families and caring adults in Gilbert and the East Valley find practical ways to get help, give help, and mentor youth. It’s nonpartisan and focused on service. You’re free to share or fork this site.
